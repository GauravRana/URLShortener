trim! - The URL shortner

URL shortening is a technique of creating shorter length web address from a specific URL(Uniform Resource Locator).


The steps needs to follow:


-Copy the url address and paste in "Enter URL" field.

-To short the web address click on "shorten!" button.

-The result is shown in the "Shortened URL" field.

-Copy or write down the result at URL search field in web browser.

-The job is done.


The example is shown here:


-Right click to copy the url

"https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/URL_shortening"

-Open trim! and paste the url on right click in "Enter URL" field.

-Press "shortern!".

-Screen will appear "Shortened URL: 25".

-Paste short URL on URL search browser.

-You are redirected to "https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/URL_shortening" .

-Well Done!
